I am a computer science MS student at Columbia University. I previously received my BA in Computer Science and Mathematics at Columbia University.

I have broad research interests in programming language theory, software systems and formal methods.

During Summer 2024, I am a visiting researcher along with Prof. Mark Santolucito at CISPA, working with Reactive Systems Group led by Prof. Bernd Finkbeiner.

Since Spring 2024, I have been working with Prof. Asaf Cidon at Columbia University, working on Linux eBPF applications.

Since Fall 2022, I have been a research assistant with Prof. Mark Santolucito at Barnard College, Columbia University. I am involved in various projects at the Barnard PL Lab, with topics spanning program synthesis, program analysis, and reactive systems.

Since Fall 2021, I have been part of the research group led by Prof. Stephen Edwards and John Hui at Columbia University, working on SSM, a deterministic real-time execution technique that allows explicit, precise timing control.
